Grand Gigs introduces 'Grand Tube' & 'Gritch', both at-home activities that can be used to earn extra money every two months! This mod also adds the option to publish a blog post from the Library or Cafe every month!

Grand Tube & Gritch:
- Minimum Age: 13
- Required Items: Computer
- Location: Home
- Post every two months

Blog Posts:
- Minimum Age: 13
- Location: Libraries & Cafes
- Post every month


(Game Version: 0.46.14)
(Mod Version: 1.0.0)
